Our Process .

We follow a systematic approach to ensure successful delivery of mining equipment and services. From initial consultation to ongoing support, our process is designed to meet your operational needs efficiently and effectively.

Consultation & Assessment

We begin by understanding your mining operation's specific equipment needs, safety requirements, and operational challenges to provide tailored solutions.

Equipment Supply & Installation

Ongoing Support & Maintenance

Trusted by Mining Operations.

Getman Equipment Excellence

Official distributor for Getman Corporation, a global supplier of mobile equipment with over 70 years of experience in customer-focused solutions for the underground mining industry. We provide comprehensive equipment sales, parts supply, and full service support across Zambia and Central Africa.

Advanced Safety Systems

MusterFire and Mantech pre-engineered fire suppression systems with 24/7 integrity monitoring, rapid detection and activation to protect your equipment and personnel. Our systems feature single and dual agent solutions, certification and compliance with world-leading accreditation bodies.

Comprehensive Workshop Services

Complete workshop component repairs, equipment rebuilds, and custom fabrication works. We offer CNC machining, plasma cutting, structural fabrication, and hydraulic pump and motor rebuilds. Mammoth exhaust systems, air intake solutions, and Multi-Wing fans for optimal equipment performance.
